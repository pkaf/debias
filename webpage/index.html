How engagements run

A predictable rhythm so stakeholders know what to expect each week.

  1. 1 Frame — goals, constraints, decision rights, and success measures.
  2. 2 Review — artefacts, interviews, and evidence as agreed in the charter.
  3. 3 Deliver — findings, options, and a practical plan with owners.
  4. 4 Transfer — optional enablement so your team can sustain the work.
Scopes are explicit, timelines are realistic, and recommendations are written to be acted on— not to impress a slide deck.
