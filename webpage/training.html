Tracks

Three places to start

Short courses and focused programs for leaders, managers, and individuals investing in their careers—covering AI literacy, decision-making under uncertainty, and the governance skills modern roles demand.

  • AI literacy for executives & boards
  • Leadership and management essentials
  • Decision rights & governance patterns

L2

Technical & data upskilling

Hands-on courses for analysts, engineers, and teams building reliable data-science capability—from foundational data literacy through to responsible use of AI in the stack. We bring the rigour; you bring real problems.

  • Data literacy for non-technical roles
  • Problem framing & evaluation design
  • Statistical literacy & honest uncertainty
  • Responsible use of LLMs in the stack
L3

Custom & enterprise programs

Bespoke curricula built around your organisation's sector, data, and decision cadence—for businesses that need something tailored rather than off-the-shelf, and teams with specific capability gaps to close.

  • Case studies from your real artefacts
  • Assessment & capability baselining
  • Post-program reinforcement plan

Questions

Common questions

Is it in-person or remote?

Both. We default to in-person for cohort work and keep remote as a first-class option with the same facilitation standards.

Do you certify?

We issue attendance and competency statements for cohort programs where assessment is part of scope. We don’t sell vanity certificates.

How do you price?

Fixed fees per format, quoted after a short scoping call. Travel and material costs are itemised separately; no surprise invoices.
