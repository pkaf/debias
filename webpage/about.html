Why we exist

Boards and operators need clarity—not buzzwords. Debias exists to shorten the distance between technical work and accountable decisions, through consulting, audit-style reviews, and training that respects your context.

What we won’t do

We don’t promise “100% accuracy,” sell proprietary black boxes you can’t explain, or substitute slide decks for evidence. Every engagement ends with something you can defend.

Start from the contact section on the home page. We’ll agree a charter, access, and reporting format before work begins—no surprise scope creep.
